FOR MY BROTHER

A Birthday Testimony

Hear this, my brother, and receive it as one receives a word set down in a book that outlives its day. There was a time when I went up to the mountain to seek the LORD, and the LORD met me there. I came back bearing fire in my chest and a burden in my hands, and many who saw me did not understand the weight I carried.

Some turned aside. Some mocked. Some said in their hearts, "This man has lost his reason." But you did not depart. You stood fast when others scattered. You kept your place when doubt circled like a storm. You did not demand proof, nor did you ask me to lay down what God had entrusted to me. You believed me, and by that belief you strengthened my hands.

As Jonathan stood beside David in the days before the throne, so you stood beside me in the days after the mountain. You were not loud, yet your presence was mighty. You were not seen by many, yet heaven took account of you.

I say to you now with a steady heart: I believe the LORD appointed you as a guardian in my life for that season. You were given to me so that I would not walk alone, so that the word I received would not be dropped in weariness or fear. You helped me hold fast to the message when the night was long and the voices around me were many.

Therefore I give you this gift, not as a thing of beauty only, but as a sign and a remembrance. The lightning that flashes, the waves that strike the rock, the figure who walks forward while others watch in awe—these are witnesses. They testify that the man who bears the fire is never truly alone, for a faithful brother stands nearby.

On this day of your birth, I bless you.

May the LORD remember your loyalty. May He honor the faith you showed in quiet places. May your strength be renewed, and may your name be written among those who stood firm when it mattered.

Know this, my brother: I have not forgotten what you did, and neither has God.

Happy birthday to you, my brother, my shield, the one who stood watch while I came down from the mountain.

Selah.
